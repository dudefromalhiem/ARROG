Guild Lore, Operations, and Classification Standard

CONFIDENTIAL

Welcome to the Guild

If you are reading this document, your account has been granted archive visibility under current Guild law. The Red Oaker Guild exists to prevent anomalous collapse events while preserving enough knowledge to keep humanity ahead of the next breach cycle.

Our doctrine is ROSED: Shelter first, Execution when absolutely necessary. We contain and study whenever viable; we terminate only when an anomaly presents irreversible existential risk. Every operation, report, and deployment traces back to this mandate.

Foundational Doctrine (ROSED)

ROSED stands for Red Oaker Shelter and Execution Department, the command philosophy that governs modern Guild operations. The Shelter half prioritizes containment, interpretation, and controlled observation. The Execution half authorizes decisive force against anomalies that cannot be stabilized without global casualty scenarios.

New personnel are expected to understand that ROSED is not contradictory. It is sequential. We shelter first. We execute only after viable containment paths have failed or become mathematically non-survivable.

Current Strategic Posture

The Guild presently operates in a stabilization cycle under Quincy-era command doctrine. Private command sovereignty is retained, MRD autonomy is high, and global anomaly response is coordinated through distributed sites with centralized archive authority.

Your role as an operator, researcher, or reviewer is to keep the archive coherent under pressure. Disorder in records leads to disorder in containment. Accuracy is part of defense.
