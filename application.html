Workshop Access
Apply to become an Editor

Editors can publish pages, upload art, and contribute media to the archive after staff review. Signed-in users can apply here. Moderators, admins, and owners already have the access they need.

What to include: a brief explanation of why you want access and any relevant work or moderation experience.
What happens next: staff review the application, then either approve editor access or leave feedback.
Already approved? use the workshop link after signing in to create submissions.

Review Notes

The staff team reviews requests manually. Applications are rate limited server-side to reduce spam and duplicate requests. If you are already a moderator or admin, the system will block the application because you already have access.

Keep your reason focused on what you will contribute to the archive.
If your request is approved, the workshop link will take you directly to page creation.
You can still browse the site and use messaging while waiting for a review.
